Our story

Some things never go out of style.

Casablanca Popcorn is inspired by the golden age of movie-going—when a night at the cinema felt like an event.

Grand Art Deco theatres, glowing marquees, velvet curtains, and the scent of freshly popped popcorn: that is the feeling we are bringing back.

Our name is inspired by Casablanca, a film that continues to be discovered, shared, and loved. It represents enduring quality and the ritual of enjoying a great film together—a standard we aspire to in every batch.

We pop with a blend of coconut and avocado oil, combining classic theatre flavour and crisp texture with carefully selected modern ingredients. No shortcuts—just quality ingredients, attention to detail, and the patience to make it properly.
